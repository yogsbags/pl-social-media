# Newsletter reference examples

Use these campaign themes and style cues (from existing creatives in this folder) to align tone, structure, and messaging when generating email newsletters.

## Example themes

- **Demat account** – Onboarding/activation; clear steps, benefits of demat; CTA to open or activate account. Professional, reassuring.
- **Dormant account** – Re-engagement; reactivate dormant accounts; gentle urgency, “we’re here when you’re ready”. Clean, minimal.
- **Groww Small Cap Fund NFO** – Product launch (NFO); key features, risk/return context, who it’s for; CTA to invest or learn more. Data-led, compliant.
- **Technical picks** – Research/insights; stock or market picks; concise, scannable; disclaimers. Analyst tone.
- **Term plan** – Insurance/product; protection, coverage, simplicity; CTA to get quote or compare. Trust, clarity.

## Style cues (from examples)

- Headlines: Short, benefit-led or curiosity-led. No jargon in the main headline.
- Body: 1–2 sentences per block; bullets where helpful. Mobile-friendly line length.
- CTAs: Single primary action per section (“Read more”, “Get started”, “Learn more”). Buttons: rounded, brand green (#00b34e).
- Tone: Professional, trustworthy, Indian financial-services. No hype or guaranteed returns.
- Compliance: Include disclaimer where needed (e.g. “Market risks apply”, “Refer to offer document”).
