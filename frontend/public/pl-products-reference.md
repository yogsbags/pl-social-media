**Type**: Flexicap Equity PMS

**Key Features**:
- India's pioneering quantamental, style-agnostic and adaptive multifactor strategy
- True-blue quant fund with systematic, rules-based approach
- Minimum Investment: INR 50 lakh
- Investment Horizon: 3-5 years minimum
- Benchmark: BSE 500 TRI
- Inception: June 12, 2023

**Performance Highlights**:
- Since Inception: 23.98% returns (vs BSE 500: 19.09%)
- 2 Year Returns: 17.80% (vs BSE 500: 19.61%)
- Strong alpha generation across market cycles
- Adaptive across market cap sizes, sectors, and styles

**Investment Philosophy**:
- Adaptive: Detects market regimes and aligns portfolio dynamically
- Quantitative: Multifactor scoring for stock selection
- Unbiased: Benchmark agnostic portfolio construction
- Alpha Focused: Periodic rebalancing and risk management

**Ideal For**:
- HNI clients seeking alpha generation
- Investors looking for systematic, emotion-free investing
- Those wanting diversified flexicap exposure
- Clients preferring quant-based approach
